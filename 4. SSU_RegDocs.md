# Start-Up & Regulatory Documents Portfolio  

SSU trackers are designed to monitor site activation readiness from pre-study to green-light activation. 

## Files  
- `SSU_Tracker_Template.xlsx` – Tool for tracking site readiness.  
- `RegDocs_Checklist.md` – Checklist of essential regulatory documents.
  
# Regulatory Documents Checklist  

- Signed Protocol  
- Investigator’s Brochure (IB)  
- Informed Consent Form (ICF)  
- PI CV & License  
- Financial Disclosure Forms  
- IRB/EC Approval Letter  
- Lab Certifications & Normal Ranges  
- Delegation of Authority Log  
- Site Training Logs

# Steps to Green Light
1. Collect all reg docs and confirm completeness
2. Prepare IRB submission package and submit
3. Receive IRB approval
4. Conduct SIV and confirm acceptable outcome
5. Issue Green Light for screening and enrollment
