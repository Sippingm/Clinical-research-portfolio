# SOW Analysis – GZOA Study  

- **Sponsor/CRO**: Eli Lilly / Fortrea  
- **Scope**: Site selection, recruitment, project management for NAFLD study.  
- **Budget**: ~$748,457.14 (6 sites, 13 patients).  
- **Key Deliverables**: Protocol review, site contracting, feasibility, PI/CRC training, recruitment strategy.  
- **Risks**: Delays in IRB approval, site activation, or drug supply.  
- **Takeaway**: SOW highlights complexity of multi-site coordination and financial forecasting.
